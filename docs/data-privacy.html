This page explains the data controls available to you within Styler, in plain language.

1. Data You Control

You can edit your name, phone number, and profile picture at any time from Profile → Edit Profile. You can add or remove saved addresses from Profile → Saved Addresses. Changes take effect immediately.

2. Notification Preferences

You can manage push notifications, email notifications, booking reminders, and promotional offers from Profile → Settings → Notifications. Your choices are saved to your account and apply across all your devices.

3. Location Data

Styler requests access to your location to surface nearby salons. Location data is used only while the app is in use and is never stored on our servers. You can revoke location permission at any time from your device settings.

4. Camera & Photos

Camera and photo library access is requested only when you upload a profile photo. No images are accessed without your explicit action.

5. Data Retention

We keep your booking history to provide support and generate personalised recommendations. You can request a full deletion of your account and associated data from Profile → Settings → Delete Account.

6. Account Deletion

Deleting your account deactivates your profile and removes it from active use within 30 days. Some transaction records may be retained for up to 7 years as required by Indian tax and financial regulations. Records retained for legal compliance will not be used for any other purpose.

7. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party providers for the following functions, all bound by strict data protection agreements:

MongoDB Atlas — database storage  |  AWS S3 — profile image storage  |  Twilio — OTP SMS delivery  |  Razorpay — payment processing  |  Firebase — push notifications

8. Security

All communication between the app and our servers is encrypted using TLS 1.2+. Passwords and tokens are never stored in plaintext. OTPs expire within 10 minutes and are single-use.

9. Contact

For data-related requests or concerns, email privacy@stylerapp.in. We respond within 72 hours.
