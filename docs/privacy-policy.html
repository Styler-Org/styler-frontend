Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your data

Privacy Policy Terms of Service Data & Privacy
Last updated: January 1, 2026

Styler ("we", "our", "us") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the rights you have over your data.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide when creating an account or using our services, including your name, phone number, email address, profile picture, and saved addresses. We also collect appointment history to help you manage your bookings.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to process bookings, send appointment reminders, personalise salon recommendations, and continuously improve our services. We may use your contact details to send service-related communications.

3. Data Sharing

We share your booking details with the salon you choose to book with so they can serve you. We never sell your personal data to third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Data Security

We use industry-standard encryption (TLS in transit, AES-256 at rest) to protect your data. Access to personal data within our systems is restricted to authorised personnel on a need-to-know basis.

5. Your Rights

You may access, update, or delete your account information at any time from the Profile section of the Styler app. You may also request a copy of your data or ask us to stop processing it by contacting us at privacy@stylerapp.in.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. If you delete your account, we will remove your personally identifiable information within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

7. Children's Privacy

Styler is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that we have collected data from a child, we will promptly delete it.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or via email. Continued use of Styler after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

9. Contact

For any privacy-related questions or concerns, please reach us at privacy@stylerapp.in.
